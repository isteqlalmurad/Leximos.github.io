About Us

Our Vision

Empowering Global Talent Bridging Language Barriers for Professional Success.

Our vision at Leximos is to transform the professional landscape for non-native English speakers by providing innovative, tailored language learning solutions. We aspire to create a world where language is no longer a barrier to career success and societal contribution, enabling every professional to unlock their full potential in the global workforce.

Our Mission

Careers through Targeted Language Mastery.

At Leximos, our mission is to empower migrants and non-native English speakers by enhancing their vocational language skills, tailored specifically to their professional needs. developing personalized learning pathways that enable our users to excel in job interviews, integrate into workplace cultures, and advance their careers in English-speaking countries. Through a combination of cutting-edge technology and community support, to promote growth and professional success.

Our Services

Vocational Language Learning

  • Customized lessons focusing on job-specific vocabulary and communication skills.
  • Real-life scenarios and simulations to practice language skills in practical contexts.

Skill Validation and Enhancement

  • Assessment tools to evaluate existing language skills and identify areas for improvement.
  • Targeted language exercises to enhance specific skills relevant to users' professional roles.

Confidence Building Activities

  • Volunteering opportunities to practice language skills in real-world settings.
  • Community engagement programs and support groups for peer-to-peer practice and encouragement.
  • Mentorship and gamification features to foster confidence and engagement in language learning.

Mobile Apps

Extending our reach with iOS and Android apps for on-the-go learning, with a web version planned.

Language Assessment

Our powerful language proficiency evaluation system uses advanced algorithms to meet users at their English level.

Our Journey

2019: Inception

Leximos began as an offline academy in Delhi, India, through our founder's collaboration with UNHCR, helping refugees gain job-specific language skills.

2023: First Prototype

Developed the initial web-based application as part of our founder's master's thesis and conducted user testing with over 20 international participants.

2023: University Recognition

Won "Best Overall Project" award from University of Dundee for real-world impact applicaton.

2023: Enterprise Challenge Win

Leximos secured first position amongst 21 teams in the Enterprise Challenge, earning £1000 in funding to advance development.

2023: Venture Competition Success:

Leximos triumphed in the Venture Competition, amongst best UK startups securing £2000 in funding to further develop its innovative language learning concept.

2024: Leximos 2.0

Began development of second iteration of leximos an advanced cloud-based version with enhanced features and mobile apps.

2024: Present Status

Leximos is incubating with the Elevator Accelerator program and collaborating closely withsocial organizations, employers and customers to refine its product offerings.
